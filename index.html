About Functional Harmony Classification

This tool classifies chords by their harmonic function based on the intervals they contain:

Tonic (stable, home)

Features:

  • Interactive chord playback with customizable waveforms
  • Chord history tracking with MIDI export (whole notes)
  • Multiple notation systems (letters, numbers, roman numerals)
  • Transposition via root/tonic selection
  • Scale filtering to find diatonic chords
  • Dynamic table controls (hide rows/columns, undo with Ctrl+Z)
